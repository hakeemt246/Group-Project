What are we about?

Dream Cakes Bakery is presenting a build your own cake bakery that allows customers to build and customize their own cakes. The icing will be available in various flavours and colours for customers to choose from. However, Dream Cakes will not be limited to providing cakes only, we will also be offering pastries and desserts. Starting Jan 31st, 2021 Dream cake will be offering the service to provide Cakes, pastries and desserts via an online platform where customers can order, track status and arrange deliveries.

Dream Cakes provides tasty and satisfying products at affordable prices. Our products will be available for sale at various locations such as supermarket and other small shops.

Our customers will feel fulfilled after consuming our products.
Dream Cakes will be located in a mall with access to all the raw materials needed, running water and electricity. Our location will also be on a bus route in a developing district.

Dream Cakes’ management team will consist of a general manager who will deal with the accounting as well as the human resource aspect of the business. The Marketing Managers to represent and promote the business and Information Technology Managers who will deal with the advertising and marketing aspect and the running of the website and orders aspect of the business respectively


Product Desirability and Demand

Due to the current changing business environment with the reality of the covid-19 pandemic there is a greater desirability and demand for a contactless business environment. Dream Cakes will be taking a proactive approach in relation to the industry since the industry at the moment still utilizes the approach where customers need to visit the store to place their orders and collect their goodies.

This approach provides a contactless way of ordering, paying and having your goodies delivered to your door as well as well as the traditional walk in system.
In this dynamic environment individuals wanted to have their cake fully customized as opposed to the basic icing and fondant flavours. Dream Cakes will also cater to individuals with gluten allergies as well as individuals who do not consume eggs.

Dream Cakes will meet the demands and desirability of the customers who are now more concerned with their health due to the current pandemic. Changes in society such as government regulations on business with social distancing protocols to name a few have resulted in the demand for business policy to change in relation to a contactless society. As a result of such changes Dream Cake will now be offering their products and services online.

Dream Cakes Bakery is a Disruptive type business model. This is based on the product and services provided. We deliver preordered cakes and pastries, but everything about the cakes is all up to you. If the dessert at a restaurant isn’t appealing order online and come down to the store, or you are given the opportunity to have a dessert at home because we deliver. You determine the size, flavor and décor of your cake making the experience given to you from us here at Dream Cakes one in a million.

Industry & Competition Analysis

Is the industry a realistic place for our new venture to enter?
The cake industry is indeed a realistic place for our new venture because despite there being a high threat to industry profitability, there will always be a high demand for baked goods as it fulfills a need for food. While there are many other substitutes for food, cakes are an important food in society, as they are traditionally associated with celebration and special occasions.

If we do enter the industry, can our firm do a better job than the industry as a whole in avoiding or diminishing the impact of the forces that suppress industry profitability?
The threats of substitute and new entrants will diminish as the online interface which allows customers to build their cake will be hard to replicate due to its capital intensive nature and unique user experience. Our novel branding and the establishment of stable distribution channels will also place us in a strong position to set ourselves apart from competitors.
